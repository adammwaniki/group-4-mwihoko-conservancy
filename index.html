We as Mwihoko Wildlife Conservative are aimed at increasing community engagement. We have employed over two hundred staffs to help in various ways of conservation thus creating a revenue stream that support many families.

We however,understand community involvement in wildlife conservation is essential for fostering a sense of responsibility, ownership, and cooperation in protecting Earth's biodiversity for future generations.
